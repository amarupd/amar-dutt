ek aisi raat ho
koi na ho kareeb hamare
sath tum or mai sirhane chand ka saya
firrr hawa bikhere teri zulfon ko
mai apni ungliyo se suljhau
baith k zameen pe falak ko dekhe hum
ek ek taare ko sath me gine hum
achanak se meri najar ka tere chehre pe ruk jaana
tere hairaan hoke bina palkon ki harkat kiye mujhe niharna
fir bijli kadke dhadam se
tu mjhko kas ke pakade araam se
mai tere mathe ko sehlate puchoo tjhe kya huwa
or tu zulfon ko kaano ke peeche lagake kahe kuchh nhi
khamosh bhari us raat ko
jazbaat shor machaye
firrr mai thamu tere hath ko
or kahu thand lag rhi h kya
tuu kanapate huwe kahe nahi itti zyada b nhi
main tujhe leke kuchhh age chalu waha
chand lakadiyaan jalake baith jaaun jaha
raat andheri sir pe chadh rhi ho
or dil ki khwahish kabi ye raat khatam na ho
jalti huyi lakadiyo pe hm apna haath seke
kabhi tu mujhe to kabhi mai tujhe dekhu
thandi hawao se tera mere kandhe pe so jana
mera kuchhh na kehna or khamosi se tujhe niharna
ek aisi raat ho koi kareeb na ho hamare 
sath tum or mai ho sirhane chand ka saya
